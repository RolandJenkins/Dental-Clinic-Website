Meet Our Owner

Dr. Meghan O'Brien

Dr. Meghan's passion for dentistry began at an early age while watching and working with her father. She is fascinated by the growth and changes in dentistry since the days her grandfather practiced. She attended the University of Minnesota Twin Cities before entering the University of Minnesota School of Dentistry through the early acceptance program. She graduated dental school in 2002 and began working at Antigo Dental Clinic with her father, Dr. Timothy O'Brien.

Dr. Meghan felt very lucky to have an amazing mentor and to practice 14 years with her father until he retired in 2016. Her passion for helping others has continued to grow in her over 20 years of practicing. "I truly love what I do. My goal is to make my patients comfortable and to help them conquer their dental fears. Preventative dentistry has not only improved the health of my patients, but has changed the philosophy of only going when it hurts."

In her free time, Dr. Meghan enjoys spending time with her husband and four young children.
